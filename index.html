Our Departments

B.Tech IT

Information Technology

An Information Technology (IT) department is the backbone of an organization's digital infrastructure. It is responsible for developing, managing, and maintaining technology assets, ensuring data security, and supporting daily technical operations so the business can run efficiently.Combines core technical skills (Data Structures, Algorithms, Web Technologies) with essential soft skills

B.Tech CSE

Computer Science

A CS Department (Computer Science Department) is an academic or corporate division focused on the study and application of computation, software, and hardware systems. It covers everything from theoretical algorithms to applied software engineering and artificial intelligence.Industry demand for skilled computer scientists.

B.Tech ECE

Electronics & Communication

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) is a core engineering discipline that focuses on designing, developing, and testing electronic equipment, semiconductor devices, and communication networks. It bridges the gap between hardware and software, driving modern advancements in smartphones, robotics, satellite communication, and artificial intelligence.

B.Tech AIML

Artificial Intelligence

AIML most commonly refers to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, a transformative field of computer science dedicated to building intelligent systems that can mimic human thought processes. It also stands for Artificial Intelligence Markup Language, an XML-based language used to build chatbots agents.

B.Tech DS

Data Science

Data science is a multidisciplinary field that combines mathematics, statistics, specialized programming, and artificial intelligence to extract actionable insights from raw data. Organizations use these insights to guide strategic planning, optimize operations, and predict future trends.

B.Tech Civil

Civil Engineering

A Civil Department is the professional, academic, or governmental division responsible for the design, construction, and maintenance of physical infrastructure including roads, bridges, water systems, and public buildings.who plan, design, construct, maintain, upgrade, and operate infrastructure like roads, bridges, etc.

About Us

Maturi Venkata Subba Rao (MVSR) Engineering College, an AUTONOMOUS institution also known as MVSR Engineering College was established in 1981 with affiliation to Osmania University. Currently, the college has B.E programs in Civil, CSE(AI/ML,DS,IoT-CS-BcT), ECE, EEE, IT, Mechanical Engineering, PG programs in Civil, CSE, ECE and Mechanical besides MBA.

MVSREC has been recognized by Osmania University as a Centre for pursuing research leading to the award of Ph.D. Degree in the Disciplines of CSE, ECE and Mechanical Engineering. In all about a 1000 students take admission per year. The college is one of the earliest of the private Engineering colleges in the state, and since inception, the college has ensured excellent and exemplary standards, which has helped the college to be one of the topmost andsought after colleges in the state.

The college is located in a sprawling campus at Nadergul, just 16 kms from the city centre. The instructional facilities are spacious, and the laboratories are continuously upgraded with state of the art equipment. There are over 200 highly qualified and dedicated faculty. Faculty of various departments handle a number of consultancy assignments from GHMC, defense laboratories etc. The college has been awarded the National Employability Award 2013 by the Aspiring minds, The UG programs in engineering have been accredited by NBA thrice. It is an ISO 9001:2015 certified Institution.About 70% of the students get jobs through campus placements in the top MNC’s like Cognizant, Infosys, Wipro, Capgemini, Deloitte, CSC, Intergraph, etc., and reputed core companies.
